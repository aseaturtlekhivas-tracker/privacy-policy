Privacy Policy for Sorter Scripts

Last Updated: May 2, 2026

1. Information We Access

Sorter Scripts only accesses your Google Sheets to perform automatic sorting and to add new rows as requested by the user. This access is required for the core functionality of the script.

2. Data Usage & Storage

We do not store, share, collect, or sell any personal data or the content of your Google Sheets. All processing happens within the Google Apps Script environment and your data never leaves the Google ecosystem.

3. Data Retention

Because we do not collect or store data, we do not retain any of your information.
